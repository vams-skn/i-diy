Game Rules

MAX SCORE: 160

Your goal is to drag and drop the items into the correct bins.

  • Each item sorted correctly gives you 10 points.
  • Each item sorted incorrectly deducts 5 points.

  • Paper: newspapers, cardboard, books, magazines, etc.
  • Glass: broken glass, empty bottles, etc.
  • Organic: fruit and vegetable peels, eggshells, tea bags, etc.
  • Plastic: plastic bottles, containers, bags, etc.

Have fun and help keep our planet clean!
